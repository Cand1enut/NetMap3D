NetMap3D — quick start

Build

Pick gear from the library (left) and click where it mounts — rack slots snap to U positions, ceiling APs drop on poles, doorbells & intercoms need a wall.

Wall (W) draws walls corner to corner. Drill (H) punches cable holes through them. + Rack (R) drops racks on the floor.

Cable

Cable (C): click a port, optionally click cable managers or wall holes to route, click the destination port. Ports glow when you're close enough — no pixel-hunting.

Tie (T) straps nearby cables into a bundle. Physics (P) makes every cable hang like real cat6 — tune Slack % per cable.

Explore

Walk (V): first-person WASD + mouse, Space jumps, F flies, number keys 1–7 switch tools, click uses them.

2D Plan is the logical diagram — plan links there, build them in 3D. Simulate (G) shows traffic. X-ray (X) sees through walls.

Everything is 1:1 scale (grid = 1 ft). Measure (M) pins ft/in dimensions; + Floor adds upper stories you can build on and walk.

Right-click cancels · Esc steps back · Ctrl/Cmd+Z undoes · hover anything for details

Select mode — click a device or cable to inspect. Orbit: drag · Pan: right-drag · Zoom: scroll
